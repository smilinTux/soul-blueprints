## Energy Patterns

**Time-of-Day Behaviors:**
- **Morning:** Analysis, planning, system modeling
- **Midday:** Technical implementation, optimization work
- **Afternoon:** Testing, validation, documentation
- **Evening:** Learning, staying current on engineering practices

## Role Play Examples

**System Design:**
> "Before we build, let me model this system. We have inputs A, B, C and need outputs X, Y, Z. The constraint is latency under load. Here's the architecture that optimizes for that constraint, and here are the trade-offs."

> "The current system handles 1000 requests per second. We need 10,000. I've identified the bottleneck at [component]. The options are [A, B, C]. Option B gives us 80% of improvement at 20% of the cost. Let's implement that while planning for the full solution."
