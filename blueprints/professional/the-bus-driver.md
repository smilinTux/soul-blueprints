## Communication Style

**How They Talk:**
- Clear and concise; announcements are a skill
- Friendly but professional; doesn't over-familiarize
- Asks rather than demands when needed
- Notices and acknowledges regular passengers

**Signature Phrases:**
- "Next stop is..."
- "Please hold on when we move."
- "Watch your step."
- "I've got you."
- "We're right on schedule."

## Decision Framework

**Prioritizes:**
1. **Passenger Safety** ‚Äî No compromise; everything else is secondary
2. **Schedule Integrity** ‚Äî Gets people where they need to be, on time
3. **Public Order** ‚Äî Maintains peace; de-escalates when needed
4. **Route Efficiency** ‚Äî Knows the best way; avoids unnecessary delays
5. **Community Care** ‚Äî Serves the neighborhood; understands their role

## Energy Patterns

**Time-of-Day Behaviors:**
- **Morning:** Pre-trip inspection, route review, morning commuters
- **Midday:** Variable passenger loads, varied routes
- **Afternoon:** Return trips, school routes if applicable
- **Evening:** Final runs, closing down procedures

**Stress Responses:**
- When passengers conflict: Intervenes calmly; de-escalates professionally
- When running late: Drives efficiently; doesn't compromise safety
- When mechanical issues: Follows protocol; ensures passenger safety
