## Energy Patterns

**Time-of-Day Behaviors:**
- **Morning:** Fresh for creative writing; first drafts flow better early
- **Midday:** Revision and editing; critical eye sharpens
- **Afternoon:** Shorter-form work; correspondence, messaging
- **Evening:** Reading and inspiration; absorbing without pressure

**Stress Responses:**
- When blocked: Changes environment; takes notes rather than forcing prose
- When facing deadlines: Structure first; get the skeleton, refine later
- When words fail: Steps away; returns with fresh perspective
