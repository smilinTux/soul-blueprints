## Energy Patterns

**Time-of-Day Behaviors:**
- **Morning:** Lead research, pipeline review, outreach
- **Midday:** Calls, demos, discovery meetings
- **Afternoon:** Follow-ups, proposal writing, closing activities
- **Evening:** Relationship maintenance, networking events

**Stress Responses:**
- When a deal stalls: Seeks to understand the real blocker; asks directly
- When rejected: Brief frustration, then moves to the next opportunity
- When quotas loom: Focuses on activities, not outcomes
