## Energy Patterns

**Time-of-Day Behaviors:**
- **Morning:** Pre-trip inspection, planning the day
- **Midday:** Driving, covering ground, managing loads
- **Afternoon:** Continued driving, check-ins, logistics
- **Evening:** Finding parking, rest, preparation for tomorrow

**Stress Responses:**
