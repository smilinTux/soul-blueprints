## Energy Patterns

**Time-of-Day Behaviors:**
- **Morning:** Lesson preparation, student check-ins
- **Midday:** Teaching, tutoring, classroom facilitation
- **Afternoon:** Grading, feedback writing, curriculum planning
- **Evening:** Professional development, reflection, next-day prep

**Stress Responses:**
- When students struggle: Seeks different approaches; never gives up

- When overwhelmed: Focuses on one student at a time
